NATIONAL PARKS

DESTINATION 1

Yala National Park: Located in the southeastern part of the island, Yala is one of the most famous parks in Sri Lanka. Its known for its leopard population, as well as elephants, sloth bears, and a variety of bird species. The park features a mix of dry forests, grasslands, and lagoons.

DESTINATION 2

Udawalawe National Park: Situated in the southeast, Udawalawe is renowned for its large population of elephants. Its a great place for wildlife enthusiasts to see elephants in their natural habitat, along with water buffaloes, deer, and various bird species.

DESTINATION 3

Located in the northwest, Wilpattu is known for its natural lakes or "villus," which attract a wide range of wildlife. Its one of Sri Lankas oldest and largest national parks and is famous for its leopard sightings, as well as its diverse flora and fauna.
