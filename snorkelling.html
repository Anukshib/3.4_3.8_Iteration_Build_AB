KANDY

WHY KANDY?

Visiting Kandy, Sri Lanka, is a journey into the heart of the island's cultural and spiritual heritage. Kandy is often referred to as the cultural capital of Sri Lanka, and for good reason.

HOT SPOTS

The town is home to the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic, one of the most revered Buddhist sites in the world. This sacred temple, believed to house a tooth of the Buddha, draws pilgrims and visitors from around the globe, especially during the annual Esala Perahera festival, where a grand procession of elephants, dancers, and drummers takes to the streets in a vibrant celebration of faith and tradition.
