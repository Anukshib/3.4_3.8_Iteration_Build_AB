CUISINE

Sri Lankan cuisine is a gem that mesmerises travellers, with its flavours and fragrant spices. When visiting, you'll be treated to an array of mouthwatering dishes that showcase the island's cultural background. Imagine indulging in a serving of rice and curry where every spoonful presents a blend of flavours from the velvety coconut milk and zesty tamarind, to the punch of chillies. Food lovers who enjoy street eats will relish in the thrill of kottu roti, a stir-fry featuring flat bread, veggies and meat seasoned to perfection.

WILD LIFE

Sri Lankan food is a culinary treasure trove that captivates tourists with its vibrant flavours and aromatic spices. As a visitor, you'll be delighted by the diverse and delicious dishes that reflect the island's rich cultural heritage. Picture yourself savouring a plate of rice and curry, where each bite offers a symphony of tastes—from the creamy coconut milk and tangy tamarind to the fiery kick of fresh chillies. Street food enthusiasts will revel in the excitement of kottu roti, a lively stir-fry of chopped flat bread, vegetables, and meat, seasoned to perfection.
