KITE SURFING

DESTINATION 1

Kalpitiya, located on the northwest coast, is the most popular kite surfing destination in Sri Lanka. Known for its strong, consistent winds from May to October and December to March, Kalpitiya offers perfect conditions for kite surfers of all levels. The Kalpitiya Lagoon is ideal for beginners, with flat, shallow water providing a safe learning environment. For more advanced kite surfers, the nearby Vella Island and Donkey Point offer exciting wave-riding opportunities.

DESTINATION 2

Mannar, located further north of Kalpitiya, is a lesser-known but equally impressive kite surfing spot. With strong winds and vast, uncrowded beaches, Mannar is perfect for those looking for a more remote and tranquil kite surfing experience. The wide-open spaces and flat water conditions make it an excellent location for freestyle kite surfing. The winds here are strong, especially from May to October, and the scenery is stunning, with a mix of lagoons, sand dunes, and historic sites nearby.

DESTINATION

While Arugam Bay is primarily famous for its surfing waves, its also a great spot for kite surfing, especially from June to September. Located on the east coast, Arugam Bay offers a more laid-back vibe with a vibrant surf culture. The winds here are generally lighter, making it suitable for beginners or those looking for a less intense kite surfing experience. The bays picturesque setting, with golden beaches and clear waters, adds to the appeal, making it a great spot to combine kite surfing with other water activities.
