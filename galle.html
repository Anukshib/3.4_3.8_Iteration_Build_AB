GALLE

WHY GALLE?

Visiting the town of Galle in Sri Lanka offers a unique blend of history, culture, and coastal beauty that is hard to find elsewhere. Walking through the cobblestone streets of Galle Fort is like stepping back in time, with its well-preserved colonial architecture, ancient churches, and historic buildings, all surrounded by massive stone ramparts that offer breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean.

HOTSPOTS

Located on the southwestern tip of the island, Galle is best known for the Galle Fort, a stunning example of a fortified city built by European colonists, in this case, the Portuguese in the 16th century and later extensively fortified by the Dutch.Galle's location also makes it an ideal base for exploring nearby attractions, such as the picturesque beaches of Unawatuna, the lush rainforests of Sinharaja, and the bustling markets of the local villages.
