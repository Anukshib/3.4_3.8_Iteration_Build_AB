ELLA

WHY ELLA?

Ella, Sri Lanka, is a must-visit destination for anyone seeking natural beauty, adventure, and a glimpse into the island's rich culture. Nestled in the heart of Sri Lanka's hill country, Ella is known for its breathtaking landscapes, with lush green tea plantations, misty mountains, and cascading waterfalls. Visitors can trek to iconic viewpoints like Ella Rock and Little Adam's Peak, where panoramic views of the surrounding countryside await.

HOT SPOTS

One of the stand-out experiences in Ella is the scenic train ride to or from Kandy, often celebrated as one of the most beautiful train journeys in the world. As the train meanders through lush tea plantations, mist-covered forests, and rolling hills, passengers are treated to breathtaking views that seem to unfold with each bend in the track. The leisurely pace of the train allows ample time to soak in the stunning landscape, from cascading waterfalls to terraced fields and charming villages.
